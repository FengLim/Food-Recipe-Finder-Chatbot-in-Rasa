Welcome To Chefbot

What is Chefbot

Chefbot is an AI chatbot which can give a suitable response to the user by message from user to help people can find the recipe they like.
It can offer the recipe with the highest matching degree based on the type of dish and the ingredients that the consumer desires.

Save your time & Bring you newness

How to Ask

Enter the (type) of recipe you want and what (ingredient) you have.
Then Chefbot will give you a list of recipe based on input given match with database.

Voice Interaction

This chatbot is more compassionate than typical recipe finder because it act like user talk to a human, not a cold computer.
To enhance its humanization, this programme also employs voice response.
